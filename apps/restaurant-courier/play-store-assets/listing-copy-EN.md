## Full description (max 4000 characters)

```
Hi courier! HIR Curier is your daily app, built so you can see orders, your route, and your earnings clearly — without digging through three screens.

WHAT YOU CAN DO

• See new orders in real time, with pickup address, customer address, and distance in km
• Accept an order with a single tap, no phone calls needed
• Follow your route on a live map, with built-in Google Maps or Waze navigation
• Chat with Hepi, the HIR AI assistant, right inside the app — ask "how many deliveries today?" or "when is my next payout?"
• Mark a delivery as completed with a photo proof
• Track your earnings per day, week and month with a clear chart
• Get push notifications when an order pops up in your zone
• Start or end your shift with one button

HOW IT WORKS

1. Log in with the email and password your HIR fleet provided
2. Start your shift when you are ready to deliver
3. Browse the feed of available orders
4. Accept the one you like
5. Drive to the restaurant, pick up the order, deliver to the customer
6. Mark "Delivered" with a photo or signature
7. Money lands in your account per your fleet's schedule (usually weekly)

WHO IS THIS APP FOR

HIR Curier is for couriers working with a HIR partner fleet or directly with a restaurant that uses the HIR platform. If you don't have an account yet, talk to your fleet or restaurant — they can onboard you in a few minutes.

This is NOT a food ordering app for customers. If you want to order food, go to your favourite restaurant that uses HIR.

SUPPORT

We've got your back. If anything goes wrong:
• Email: curier@hirforyou.ro
• Web: curier.hirforyou.ro/contact
• In-app chat with Hepi, available around the clock

We reply within a few hours during business hours.

PRIVACY AND PERMISSIONS

The app uses your location during your active shift so it can calculate route and ETA for customers. Your location is NOT shared with anyone outside your fleet and stops automatically when you end your shift.

We use the camera only for delivery proof photos (optional). Push notifications are for new orders and dispatcher messages.

Full privacy policy: curier.hirforyou.ro/privacy
Terms of service: curier.hirforyou.ro/terms
Account deletion: curier.hirforyou.ro/settings/delete-account

HIR is a Romanian platform for restaurants and couriers, launched in 2026. We focus on simplicity and on getting your money on time.

Good luck out there!
```
